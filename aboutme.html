So just who in the world am I?

I'm Brandon! I'm a guy that loves to learn as much as I love to put what I learn to use. I studied Chemistry for two years at Salt Lake Community College, and when you couldn't find me in class, you could find me with a book on Computer Science (a passion I had yet to ignite). Programming has been a minor hobby of mine for years, dabbling in everything from C to Python, but I soon realized how perfect this field would be to always keep my mind sharp.

On the less nerdy side of things, I love being surrounded by nature and getting *safely* lost in the woods. Nothing clears your head better than going back to your roots and surrounding yourself with the beautiful wilderness.
And thunderstorms.
Gotta love thunderstorms.
